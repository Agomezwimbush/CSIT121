My Favorite Books!


I love to read books when I have the time. Some of my favorite books that I have read so far are the House of Night series by P.C. and Kristen Cast. Which is about a newly formed vampire trying to learn how to deal with the chnage she just went through. If you like vampire books that are NOT Twilight, this is a really good read, espcially for a young adult series. Another book that I loved was Ready Player One by Earnest Cline. I got this book in a Loot Crate box and immediately loved it. I geeked out with all of the video game refrencences!
